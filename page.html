Welcome to

AARADHYA

AARADHYA in Buddhism means the victory of the spirit over the senses of intelligence and wisdom of knowledge. It symbolizes enlightenment, wisdom, and knowledge.

Our team aims to provide an environment to remain fit and strong both physically and mentally. It is best way to reduce the areas of anxiety, depression etc. We believe, meditation is a way of self-transformation through self-observation which focuses on the deep interconnection between mind and body, which can be experienced directly by disciplined attention to the physical sensations that form the life of the body. Our mission at AARADHYA is to make people aware about the benefits of meditation, bringing with it inner peace.

Are you ready for
meditation?

1. Sit or lie comfortably.

2. Close your eyes.

3. Make no effort to control the breath; simply breathe naturally.

4. Focus your attention on the breath and on how the body moves with
each inhalation and exhalation.

5.Notice the movement of your body as you breathe.

6.If your mind wanders, simply return your focus back to your breath.

Our Training Classes

Yoga Training

The word “Yoga” essentially means, “that which brings you to reality”. Literally, it means “union.” Union means it brings you to the ultimate reality, where individual manifestations of life are surface bubbles in the process of creation.


Cardio Training

Cardio Yoga is a type of yoga class that combines Yoga moves with cardiovascular exercises. The workout takes classic yoga moves and makes them a bit faster, with a more rapid flow between sequences. The non-stop movement gets your heart beating and your muscles burning.
